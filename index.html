ABOUT US

Peer Connect is a student run mentorship company that provides peer mentorship services to JEE aspirants We are providing a peer mentorship program. That happens all across the globe in universities like Harvard,companies like JP Morgan. This is a tried and tested method of mentorship but unfortunately does not happen for JEE students. We are trying to bridge this gap . We are matching students in 11th and 12th with students who are doing very well in their colleges and who are perfect to guide and mentor the current batch. The Seniors have seen the same problems these juniors go through and can help them with their own personal experience.

WHAT WE OFFER

Peer Connect’s Peer Mentoring scheme brings together students from different year groups to form one-to-one mentoring relationships. By committing a small amount of time to mentoring every week, you can really get ahead and maximise and realise your potential in life Peer Mentoring is a voluntary scheme which is flexible, confidential, and fits around your availability and preferences. Training, resources and ongoing support are provided by our dedicated team

BENEFITS OF BEING A MENTEE

    Help form a good temperament

    Better deal with your socio-academic issues

    Develop strategies for deapng with both personal and academic issues

    Gain valuable insight into the next stage of your university career

    Become more empowered to make decisions 

    Have better knowledge and understanding to make your career decisions.
