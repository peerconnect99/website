ABOUT US

Peer Connect is a student run mentorship company that provides peer mentorship services to JEE aspirants We are providing a peer mentorship program. That happens all across the globe in universities like Harvard,companies like JP Morgan. This is a tried and tested method of mentorship but unfortunately does not happen for JEE students. We are trying to bridge this gap . We are matching students in 11th and 12th with students who are doing very well in their colleges and who are perfect to guide and mentor the current batch. The Seniors have seen the same problems these juniors go through and can help them with their own personal experience.
